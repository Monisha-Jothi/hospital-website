⚕️ General Medicine Department

Your trusted partner for comprehensive primary care, preventive medicine, and the diagnosis and treatment of common health conditions

Comprehensive Primary Care

Our General Medicine Department serves as the foundation of your healthcare journey. Our board-certified internists and family medicine physicians provide comprehensive primary care for adults, focusing on prevention, early detection, and management of common and complex medical conditions.

We believe in building long-term relationships with our patients, getting to know your health history, lifestyle, and goals to provide personalized care that keeps you healthy throughout your life. Whether you need a routine checkup, management of chronic conditions, or diagnosis of new symptoms, we're here for you.

Our General Medicine Services

We offer a comprehensive range of primary care services:

  • Annual physical examinations and health screenings
  • Preventive care and wellness counseling
  • Acute illness diagnosis and treatment
  • Chronic disease management
  • Vaccination and immunization services
  • Health risk assessments
  • Pre-operative medical clearance
  • Travel medicine and vaccinations
  • Women's health services
  • Men's health services
  • Geriatric medicine
  • Minor procedures and wound care
  • Laboratory testing and diagnostic services
  • Medication management and review
  • Specialist referrals and care coordination
  • Same-day appointments for urgent needs

Common Conditions We Treat

Our general medicine physicians diagnose and treat a wide variety of health conditions:

  • Hypertension (high blood pressure)
  • Diabetes (Type 1 and Type 2)
  • High cholesterol and lipid disorders
  • Obesity and weight management
  • Thyroid disorders
  • Respiratory infections and chronic lung disease
  • Asthma and COPD
  • Digestive disorders and gastrointestinal issues
  • Urinary tract infections
  • Skin conditions and rashes
  • Arthritis and joint pain
  • Anemia and blood disorders
  • Osteoporosis
  • Anxiety and depression
  • Sleep disorders
  • Allergies
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Fatigue and weakness

Preventive Care & Wellness

Prevention is the cornerstone of good health. Our preventive care services are designed to detect health issues early and help you maintain optimal wellness:

  • Comprehensive health screenings based on age and risk factors
  • Cancer screenings (colorectal, breast, cervical, prostate)
  • Cardiovascular risk assessment
  • Diabetes screening and prevention
  • Bone density testing
  • Immunizations for adults (flu, pneumonia, shingles, etc.)
  • Nutrition and diet counseling
  • Exercise and fitness guidance
  • Smoking cessation programs
  • Alcohol and substance use counseling
  • Stress management techniques
  • Sleep hygiene education

Chronic Disease Management

Living with a chronic condition requires ongoing care and support. Our chronic disease management programs help you:

  • Control symptoms and prevent complications
  • Optimize medication regimens
  • Monitor disease progression
  • Make lifestyle modifications
  • Coordinate care with specialists
  • Achieve treatment goals

We provide comprehensive management for conditions like diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, asthma, COPD, arthritis, and many others, with regular follow-ups and adjustments to your treatment plan as needed.

Annual Physical Examinations

Regular checkups are essential for maintaining good health and catching problems early. During your annual physical, we will:

  • Review your medical history and current medications
  • Perform a comprehensive physical examination
  • Check vital signs and body measurements
  • Order appropriate screening tests and lab work
  • Discuss preventive care recommendations
  • Address any health concerns or questions
  • Update immunizations as needed
  • Create or update your personalized health plan

Women's & Men's Health

We provide gender-specific health services addressing the unique needs of women and men:

Women's Health: Well-woman exams, Pap smears, breast exams, contraception counseling, menopause management, osteoporosis screening, and more.

Men's Health: Prostate health screening, testosterone level evaluation, erectile dysfunction treatment, cardiovascular risk assessment, and more.

Geriatric Medicine

As we age, our healthcare needs change. Our geriatric medicine services address the special needs of older adults, including:

  • Comprehensive geriatric assessments
  • Fall prevention and balance evaluation
  • Memory screening and cognitive assessment
  • Medication management and polypharmacy review
  • Advance care planning
  • Coordination with home health services
  • Quality of life optimization

Your Health Partner

Our general medicine physicians serve as your primary point of contact for all your healthcare needs. We coordinate with specialists when necessary, manage your overall care, and help you navigate the healthcare system. Think of us as your health partner, advocate, and guide on your wellness journey.
