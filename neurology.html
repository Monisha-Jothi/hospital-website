Comprehensive Neurological Care

Our Neurology Department provides expert diagnosis and treatment for the full spectrum of neurological conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. Our board-certified neurologists combine deep clinical expertise with the most advanced diagnostic technology available.

We take a compassionate, patient-centered approach — working closely with you and your family to develop treatment plans that address not just symptoms, but your overall quality of life.

Conditions We Treat

  • Stroke and transient ischemic attacks (TIA)
  • Epilepsy and seizure disorders
  • Multiple sclerosis and demyelinating diseases
  • Parkinson's disease and movement disorders
  • Alzheimer's disease and other dementias
  • Migraine headaches and chronic headache disorders
  • Peripheral neuropathy and nerve damage
  • Myasthenia gravis and neuromuscular disorders
  • Vertigo, balance disorders, and Bell's palsy
  • Traumatic brain injury and spinal cord disorders

Stroke Warning Signs — Act F.A.S.T.

  • Face drooping suddenly
  • Arm weakness on one side
  • Speech difficulty or slurring
  • Severe sudden headache
  • Vision changes or loss
  • Balance or coordination loss

Time is critical — call 911 immediately!

When to See a Neurologist

  • Severe or chronic headaches
  • Seizures or blackouts
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Memory problems
  • Tremors or balance issues
  • Vision or speech problems
