Comprehensive Neurological Care

Our Neurology Department provides expert diagnosis and treatment for the full spectrum of neurological conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. Our team of board-certified neurologists combines clinical expertise with advanced technology to deliver personalized, effective care.

We understand that neurological conditions can be complex and life-altering. That's why we take a compassionate, patient-centered approach, working closely with you and your family to develop treatment plans that address not just symptoms, but your overall quality of life.

Our Neurology Services

We offer comprehensive diagnostic and treatment services for all types of neurological disorders:

  • Comprehensive neurological examinations and assessments
  • Electroencephalography (EEG) for seizure and brain activity monitoring
  • Electromyography (EMG) and nerve conduction studies
  • Advanced neuroimaging including MRI and CT scans
  • Lumbar puncture and cerebrospinal fluid analysis
  • Sleep studies for sleep-related neurological disorders
  • Botox therapy for migraines, spasticity, and movement disorders
  • Infusion therapy for multiple sclerosis and other conditions
  • Cognitive and memory assessments
  • Stroke prevention and management programs
  • Headache and migraine treatment center
  • Movement disorder clinic
  • Epilepsy monitoring and treatment
  • Neuromuscular disease clinic

Advanced Diagnostic Technology

Accurate diagnosis is crucial for effective neurological treatment. Our department features state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment:

  • High-resolution 3T MRI scanner for detailed brain and spine imaging
  • Advanced CT angiography for vascular studies
  • Digital EEG with video monitoring for comprehensive seizure evaluation
  • EMG/NCS equipment for precise neuromuscular assessment
  • Transcranial Doppler ultrasound for cerebrovascular evaluation
  • Evoked potential testing for sensory pathway assessment
  • Polysomnography lab for sleep disorder diagnosis
  • Neuropsychological testing facilities

Specialized Neurology Programs

We offer specialized programs for complex neurological conditions:

Stroke Center

Our comprehensive stroke center provides rapid evaluation and treatment for acute stroke, including thrombolytic therapy and mechanical thrombectomy. We also offer extensive stroke prevention services and rehabilitation programs.

Headache and Migraine Clinic

Specialized care for chronic headaches and migraines, including preventive treatments, acute management strategies, and innovative therapies like Botox injections and nerve blocks.

Movement Disorder Center

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment for Parkinson's disease, essential tremor, dystonia, and other movement disorders, with access to the latest medical and surgical therapies.

Memory and Cognitive Disorders Clinic

Specialized assessment and management of Alzheimer's disease, dementia, and memory problems, with comprehensive cognitive testing and support for patients and caregivers.

Meet Our Neurology Team

Our neurology team includes board-certified neurologists with fellowship training in various subspecialties, including stroke, epilepsy, movement disorders, neuromuscular diseases, and headache medicine. Many of our physicians are actively involved in clinical research and teaching.

We work closely with neurosurgeons, neuroradiologists, rehabilitation specialists, and other healthcare professionals to ensure comprehensive, coordinated care for complex neurological conditions.

Research and Innovation

Our department is committed to advancing neurological care through clinical research. We participate in clinical trials for new treatments and technologies, giving our patients access to cutting-edge therapies that may not yet be widely available.

When to See a Neurologist

  • Severe or chronic headaches
  • Seizures or loss of consciousness
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Muscle weakness
  • Tremors or movement problems
  • Memory problems
  • Dizziness or balance issues
  • Vision problems

Stroke Warning Signs

  • Sudden face drooping
  • Arm weakness
  • Speech difficulty
  • Severe headache
  • Vision changes
  • Balance problems

Time is critical - call 911 immediately!
