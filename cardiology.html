Advanced Heart Care Excellence

Our Cardiology Department is dedicated to preventing, diagnosing, and treating all forms of cardiovascular disease. With a team of board-certified cardiologists, cutting-edge diagnostic equipment, and comprehensive treatment options, we provide personalized care for every heart condition.

We believe in a holistic approach to heart health — combining the latest medical treatments with lifestyle counseling, rehabilitation, and ongoing support tailored to you.

Conditions We Treat

  • Coronary artery disease and atherosclerosis
  • Heart attack (myocardial infarction) — acute and post-care
  • Heart failure and cardiomyopathy
  • Arrhythmias including atrial fibrillation
  • Valvular heart disease (stenosis and regurgitation)
  • High blood pressure and hypertensive heart disease
  • Peripheral artery disease and aortic aneurysms
  • Congenital heart defects in adults
  • Chest pain, angina, and pericardial disease

State-of-the-Art Technology

Our cardiology department features the most advanced technology for precise, non-invasive diagnoses and minimally invasive treatments — including a 256-slice cardiac CT, advanced 3D echocardiography, digital cardiac cath lab with rotational angiography, intravascular ultrasound (IVUS), and fractional flow reserve (FFR) technology.

Cardiac Rehabilitation Program

Recovery from a cardiac event is a journey, and we're with you every step. Our personalised rehab program combines supervised exercise, nutrition counseling, stress management, and education — helping you regain strength, reduce risk factors, and improve quality of life.

Warning Signs

  • Chest pain or tightness
  • Shortness of breath
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Family history of heart disease
