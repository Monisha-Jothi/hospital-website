❤️ Cardiology Department

Comprehensive cardiovascular care combining expertise, advanced technology, and compassionate treatment for optimal heart health

Advanced Heart Care Excellence

Our Cardiology Department is dedicated to preventing, diagnosing, and treating all forms of cardiovascular disease. With a team of board-certified cardiologists, cutting-edge diagnostic equipment, and comprehensive treatment options, we provide personalized care for every heart condition.

Whether you're dealing with a complex cardiac condition or seeking preventive care, our cardiology team offers the expertise and compassion you need. We believe in a holistic approach to heart health, combining medical treatments with lifestyle counseling and ongoing support.

Our Cardiology Services

We offer a complete range of cardiovascular services, from routine screenings to advanced interventional procedures:

  • Comprehensive cardiovascular risk assessment and prevention programs
  • Non-invasive cardiac testing including EKG, echocardiography, and stress testing
  • Advanced cardiac imaging with CT angiography and cardiac MRI
  • 24-hour Holter monitoring and event recorders for arrhythmia detection
  • Coronary angiography and cardiac catheterization procedures
  • Interventional cardiology including angioplasty and stent placement
  • Heart failure management and treatment programs
  • Arrhythmia diagnosis and treatment including ablation procedures
  • Pacemaker and defibrillator implantation and monitoring
  • Valvular heart disease diagnosis and management
  • Hypertension management and medication optimization
  • Lipid management and cholesterol treatment
  • Cardiac rehabilitation programs
  • Pre-operative cardiac clearance evaluations

Conditions We Treat

Our cardiologists have extensive experience treating a wide range of heart conditions:

  • Coronary artery disease and atherosclerosis
  • Heart attack (myocardial infarction) acute and post-care
  • Heart failure and cardiomyopathy
  • Heart valve disorders including stenosis and regurgitation
  • Arrhythmias including atrial fibrillation and ventricular tachycardia
  • Congenital heart defects in adults
  • Peripheral artery disease
  • High blood pressure and hypertensive heart disease
  • High cholesterol and lipid disorders
  • Aortic aneurysms and dissections
  • Pericardial disease
  • Chest pain and angina

State-of-the-Art Technology

Our cardiology department features the latest medical technology to ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatments:

  • Advanced 3D echocardiography for detailed heart imaging
  • 256-slice cardiac CT scanner for non-invasive coronary imaging
  • Cardiac MRI for comprehensive structural and functional assessment
  • Digital cardiac catheterization lab with rotational angiography
  • Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) for precise vessel assessment
  • Fractional flow reserve (FFR) technology for optimal stent placement
  • Electrophysiology lab for complex arrhythmia mapping and ablation
  • Remote cardiac monitoring systems for continuous patient care

Meet Our Cardiology Team

Our team consists of highly trained cardiologists, interventional specialists, electrophysiologists, and cardiovascular nurses who work collaboratively to provide exceptional care. Each physician is board-certified and maintains active involvement in cardiology research and continuing medical education.

We believe in a team-based approach to cardiac care, bringing together specialists from various disciplines to develop comprehensive treatment plans tailored to each patient's unique needs.

Cardiac Rehabilitation Program

Recovery from a cardiac event or procedure is a journey, and we're with you every step of the way. Our cardiac rehabilitation program combines supervised exercise, nutrition counseling, stress management, and education to help you regain strength, reduce risk factors, and improve your quality of life.

The program is personalized to your specific condition and fitness level, with experienced cardiac rehabilitation specialists monitoring your progress and adjusting your plan as needed.

Prevention & Wellness

Prevention is the best medicine for heart disease. We offer comprehensive cardiovascular risk assessments that evaluate your family history, lifestyle factors, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and other risk factors to create a personalized prevention plan.

Our preventive cardiology services include dietary counseling, exercise prescription, smoking cessation support, stress management techniques, and medication management to help you maintain optimal heart health and prevent future cardiovascular problems.
