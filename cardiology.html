Cardiology Department

The Cardiology Department specializes in diagnosing and treating heart-related diseases using advanced medical technology and expert care.

Conditions We Treat

  • High Blood Pressure (Hypertension)
  • Heart Attack (Myocardial Infarction)
  • Chest Pain & Angina
  • Irregular Heartbeat (Arrhythmia)
  • Heart Valve Disorders
  • Congenital Heart Defects
  • Heart Failure

Treatments & Solutions

  • ECG & 2D Echo Diagnostic Tests
  • Medication Therapy & Lifestyle Guidance
  • Angiography & Angioplasty
  • Pacemaker Implantation
  • Heart Valve Repair / Replacement
  • Emergency Cardiac Care (24x7 ICU)
