Our Story

Three Decades of
Changing Lives

Founded in 1995 by a group of visionary physicians, MediCare Excellence began with a simple mission: to provide world-class healthcare that combines cutting-edge medical technology with genuine human compassion.

What started as a small community hospital has grown into a comprehensive healthcare network, serving over 25,000 patients annually and earning recognition as the region's most trusted medical institution.

1995
Founded as MediCare ClinicOpened with 5 doctors and a vision for community care
2003
Expanded to Full HospitalAdded 200 beds, surgical wing, and ICU facilities
2012
JCI Accreditation AchievedFirst in the region to earn international quality certification
2020
Launched Telehealth PlatformBringing expert care to patients anywhere, anytime
2024
Robotic Surgery Center OpenedState-of-the-art robotic-assisted surgical facility launched

What We Stand For
