Caring for Your Child's Health

Our Pediatrics Department is dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare for children from birth through adolescence. We understand that children have unique medical needs requiring specialized care, patience, and a gentle touch.

Our board-certified pediatricians create a warm, welcoming environment where children feel comfortable and families feel supported. We partner with parents to ensure every child receives the personalized care they need to grow healthy and strong.

When to Call or Visit Immediately

  • High fever in infant under 3 months
  • Difficulty breathing or rapid breathing
  • Severe dehydration (no urination, dry mouth)
  • Head injury with vomiting
  • Allergic reaction or hives
  • Severe or unusual pain
