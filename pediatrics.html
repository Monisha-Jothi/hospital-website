Common Conditions We Treat

Our pediatricians have expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of childhood illnesses and conditions:

  • Respiratory infections (colds, flu, bronchitis, pneumonia)
  • Ear infections and earaches
  • Strep throat and tonsillitis
  • Asthma and allergies
  • Eczema and other skin conditions

  • Urinary tract infections

  • Growth and developmental concerns
  • Behavioral issues and sleep problems

  • Anxiety and depression in children and teens
  • Diabetes and endocrine disorders
  • Sports injuries

Well-Child Care

Regular well-child visits are essential for monitoring your child's growth, development, and overall health. These appointments allow us to:

  • Track growth and development milestones
  • Provide age-appropriate immunizations
  • Screen for developmental delays or health concerns
  • Discuss nutrition, safety, and parenting questions
  • Build a trusting relationship with your family
  • Identify potential health issues early
  • Provide guidance on age-specific health topics

We follow the American Academy of Pediatrics recommended schedule for well-child visits, with more frequent visits in infancy and annually thereafter.

Newborn and Infant Care

The first year of life is a time of rapid growth and development. Our newborn care services include:

  • Hospital visits for newborns
  • First week newborn checkup
  • Breastfeeding support and lactation consultation
  • Newborn screening and testing
  • Jaundice monitoring and treatment
  • Circumcision care
  • Feeding and sleep guidance
  • Infant safety counseling

Adolescent Medicine

The teenage years bring unique health challenges. Our adolescent medicine services provide confidential, respectful care addressing:

  • Puberty and hormonal changes
  • Acne treatment
  • Mental health screening and support
  • Sports medicine and injury prevention
  • Nutritional counseling and eating disorders
  • Sexual health education
  • College health preparation
  • Substance abuse prevention

Childhood Immunizations

Vaccines are one of the most important ways to protect your child's health. We provide all recommended childhood vaccines and take time to answer parents' questions about vaccine safety and schedules. Our vaccine refrigerators are monitored 24/7 to ensure proper storage and effectiveness.

Chronic Disease Management

For children with chronic conditions like asthma, diabetes, or allergies, we provide comprehensive ongoing care including regular monitoring, medication management, education for families, and coordination with specialists when needed.

Child-Friendly Environment

We've designed our pediatric department to be welcoming and fun for children. Our waiting area features toys, books, and activities to keep kids entertained. Exam rooms are decorated with bright colors and child-friendly themes. We strive to make every visit as stress-free as possible for both children and parents.

Meet Our Pediatric Team

Our pediatric team includes board-certified pediatricians, pediatric nurse practitioners, and specialized nursing staff trained in working with children. We believe in building long-term relationships with families, often caring for children from birth through college.
