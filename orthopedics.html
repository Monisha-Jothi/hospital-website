Expert Musculoskeletal Care

Our Orthopedics Department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions affecting bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. From sports injuries to complex joint replacements, our team delivers outstanding outcomes using the latest minimally invasive techniques.

We believe recovery begins before surgery — with accurate diagnosis, careful planning, and a comprehensive rehabilitation program to get you back to the life you love.

Conditions We Treat

  • Arthritis — osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis
  • ACL tears, meniscus tears, and ligament injuries
  • Rotator cuff tears and shoulder instability
  • Herniated discs, spinal stenosis, and scoliosis
  • Fractures, dislocations, and sports injuries
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome and plantar fasciitis
  • Bunions, hammertoes, and foot deformities
  • Osteoporosis and bone density disorders
