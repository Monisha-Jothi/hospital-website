Expert Musculoskeletal Care

Our Orthopedics Department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions affecting your bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. Whether you're dealing with a sports injury, chronic joint pain, or need a joint replacement, our team of board-certified orthopedic surgeons provides expert care using the latest techniques.

We believe in a comprehensive approach to orthopedic care that begins with accurate diagnosis, considers all treatment options from conservative to surgical, and includes comprehensive rehabilitation to help you return to the activities you love.

Our Orthopedic Services

We offer a full range of orthopedic services to address all musculoskeletal conditions:

  • Comprehensive orthopedic evaluations and second opinions
  • Advanced diagnostic imaging including X-ray, MRI, and CT scans
  • Sports medicine and athletic injury treatment
  • Joint replacement surgery (hip, knee, shoulder, ankle)
  • Arthroscopic and minimally invasive surgery
  • Fracture care and trauma surgery
  • Spine surgery and spinal disorders treatment
  • Hand and wrist surgery
  • Foot and ankle surgery
  • Shoulder and elbow surgery
  • Pediatric orthopedics
  • Arthritis management and treatment
  • Osteoporosis screening and treatment
  • Physical therapy and rehabilitation
  • Orthobiologics including PRP and stem cell therapy
  • Cartilage restoration procedures

Conditions We Treat

Our orthopedic specialists treat a wide variety of musculoskeletal conditions:

  • Arthritis (osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis)
  • ACL tears and other ligament injuries
  • Meniscus tears
  • Rotator cuff tears
  • Tennis elbow and golfer's elbow
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Plantar fasciitis
  • Herniated discs and spinal stenosis
  • Fractures and broken bones
  • Dislocations
  • Bursitis and tendinitis
  • Bunions and hammertoes
  • Hip labral tears
  • Bone spurs
  • Scoliosis and spinal deformities
  • Sports-related injuries
  • Work-related injuries
  • Chronic pain conditions

Advanced Surgical Techniques

Our orthopedic surgeons are trained in the latest surgical techniques to minimize recovery time and maximize outcomes:

  • Minimally invasive arthroscopic procedures
  • Robotic-assisted joint replacement surgery
  • Computer-navigated spine surgery
  • Anterior approach hip replacement
  • Partial knee replacement
  • Reverse shoulder replacement
  • Minimally invasive spinal fusion
  • Cartilage transplantation
  • Ligament reconstruction
  • Tendon repair and transfer

Joint Replacement Center

Our Joint Replacement Center is dedicated to helping patients with severe arthritis or joint damage regain mobility and quality of life. We perform hundreds of successful joint replacements each year with excellent outcomes.

Features of our Joint Replacement program include:

  • Pre-operative education classes
  • Minimally invasive surgical techniques
  • Enhanced recovery protocols
  • Dedicated orthopedic floor with specialized nursing care
  • Comprehensive physical therapy
  • Same-day and outpatient joint replacement options
  • Long-term follow-up care

Sports Medicine

Our sports medicine specialists work with athletes of all levels, from weekend warriors to professional competitors. We provide comprehensive care for sports-related injuries and work to get you back in the game safely and quickly.

Services include injury prevention assessments, performance optimization, acute injury treatment, surgical repair when necessary, and sport-specific rehabilitation programs.

Rehabilitation Services

Recovery doesn't end when you leave the operating room. Our comprehensive rehabilitation program is designed to help you regain strength, mobility, and function. Our physical therapists work closely with your surgeon to create personalized recovery plans.

We offer on-site physical therapy with state-of-the-art equipment, aquatic therapy, occupational therapy, and home exercise programs to support your recovery journey.

Meet Our Orthopedic Team

Our team includes board-certified orthopedic surgeons with fellowship training in specialized areas such as sports medicine, joint replacement, spine surgery, hand surgery, and foot and ankle surgery. Many of our surgeons serve as team physicians for local schools and sports organizations.

Supporting our surgeons is a dedicated team of physician assistants, nurse practitioners, physical therapists, and specialized nursing staff, all committed to providing exceptional orthopedic care.
